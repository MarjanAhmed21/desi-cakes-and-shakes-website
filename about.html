About Us

Handcrafted bakes made with love in Oldham

Our Story

We started as a small home bakery, creating fresh desserts for family and friends. Over time, our passion grew into a full business serving custom cakes, desserts, and treats across Oldham and beyond.

Everything we make is freshly prepared, with attention to detail and quality ingredients. Whether it’s a birthday cake or a small sweet box, we put care into every order.

Desi shakes and cakes is established over 13 years. We specialise in cakes for all occasions. Milshakes, waffles, desserts, crepes. Falooda, ice cream and many more. Give us try and you will be back for more!
